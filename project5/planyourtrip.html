Planning your trip to Aruba is exciting, and we're here to help you make the most of your visit. From finding the perfect place to stay to navigating the island, we've got all the essentials covered.

Accommodations:

When planning your stay in Aruba, consider booking directly with the hotel. While third-party booking sites may offer convenience, booking directly often provides additional benefits such as room upgrades, flexible cancellation policies, and special discounts. However, if you're simply browsing your options, a third-party booking site can be a useful tool to explore the available selections. Take a look here for a comprehensive list of accommodations in Aruba.

Transportation:

Getting around Aruba is easy and convenient. Consider renting a car from local companies, as they often offer more competitive rates compared to larger chains like Hertz or Enterprise. With your own vehicle, you can explore the island at your own pace. Alternatively, opt for public transportation for an authentic experience, or book a guided tour to discover the best sights hassle-free.

Travel Tips:

Best time to visit: Enjoy year-round sunshine, with the best weather from January to March.
Currency: Aruban Florin (AWG), but US dollars are widely accepted.
Safety: Aruba is one of the safest islands in the Caribbean, but always take common precautions.
